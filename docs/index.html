zkYearn Finance

zkYearn Finance (zkYFI) is the fork of Yearn Finance (YFI) that will soon include zkRollup tech via zkSync.
zkYFI will have Staking, Liquidity Mining, and Yield Farming.

With zkYFI once zkRollup tech is integrated all transactions will happen privately on L2 ETH which will result in
Privacy, Fast transactions, and lower fees
